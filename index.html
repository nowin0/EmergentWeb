Emergent

Autonomous protein design for biology's hardest failure modes.

Emergent models protein mutation with graph neural networks, develops new architectures for structural biology, and aims those systems at biotech and pharma use cases where misfolding drives disease. The long-term ambition is not just to read molecular failure, but to design around it.

Mutation intelligence

Structure-native learning

Therapeutic direction
